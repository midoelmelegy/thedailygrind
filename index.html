We are doing more than you expect

The Daily Grind was established in December 2008 as Kuala Lumpur’s very first gourmet burger restaurant dedicated to serving a range of crafted gourmet burgers for the adventurous. Our vegetarian options are every bit as yummy too!

Made from 100% prime beef, lamb and chicken, our patties use the freshest ingredients, to complement our artisanal home-made sauces. We buy only premium cuts of chilled imported halal meat and grind them on the premises in a temperature-controlled process. All patties are then weighed and moulded, enabling us to completely control the patty-making process and to be absolutely certain of the quality and freshness of everything we make.
